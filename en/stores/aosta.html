The Store

Where it all began

Opened in 1988 in the heart of Aosta, this is the store that gave birth to Gal Sport. Here you'll find our deepest expertise: a team of experts who know every slope in Aosta Valley and can recommend the perfect equipment.

Our professional workshop is our pride: waxing, edge tuning and repairs performed with state-of-the-art machinery.
