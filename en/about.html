Our Story

Since 1988 in Aosta Valley

We were born in Aosta when Aosta Valley was becoming one of Europe's most beloved ski destinations. Since then, we have accompanied generations of athletes on their mountain adventures, growing together with the territory we love.

Our Journey

1988

The Beginning

We opened our first store on Via Paravera in Aosta. A small space with a great passion for skiing and mountains.

2001

On the Pila Slopes

Expansion with our first rental point directly on the slopes, to be even closer to skiers.

2004

The Showroom

The large Pollein showroom was born: 1000sqm dedicated to sports, with a bike workshop and extensive display.

2017

Cogne

We opened in Cogne, in the heart of Gran Paradiso Park, with a focus on e-bikes and summer hiking.

2021

Cervinia

The sixth store arrived at the foot of the Matterhorn: equipment rental and storage for one of the most spectacular ski areas in the Alps.

Today

6 Stores, 1 Passion

With 6 stores throughout Aosta Valley, we are the largest sports store network in the region. And we continue to grow.
