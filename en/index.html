About Us

Passion for the mountains,
since 1988

We were born in Aosta when Aosta Valley was becoming one of Europe's most beloved ski destinations. Since then, we have accompanied generations of athletes on their adventures.

Today with 6 stores we are always close to your favorite slopes: from Pila to Cervinia, from Cogne to Crevacol.
